This is the first of 5 test pages. Follow the steps below to test the "Save & Next" feature.

1
Open Auto-Pagination Settings

Click the extension icon and open the Auto-Pagination settings page.

2
Configure the Next Button Selector

Enter this selector: .next-btn

3
Save Configuration

Click "Save Configuration" button

4
Inject the Script

Open the browser console (F12) and run:

chrome.tabs.query({active: true}, tabs => {
  chrome.scripting.executeScript({
    target: {tabId: tabs[0].id},
    files: ['autoPagination.js']
  })
})

Or send a message to start auto-pagination:

chrome.tabs.query({active: true}, tabs => {
  chrome.tabs.sendMessage(tabs[0].id, {action: 'startAutoPagination'})
})
5
Look for the "Save & Next" Button

A purple floating button should appear in the bottom-right corner

6
Save This Page

Click the extension icon and click "Save Page" to save this content to Notion

7
Click "Save & Next"

After saving, click the floating "Save & Next" button. It will automatically click the button below and navigate to Page 2!
